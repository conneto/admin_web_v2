Introduce:
SUGAR Vietnam is a non-profit organization established in 2008 by Vietnamese international students in Singapore. We operate with a mission to support children in shelters with the opportunity to reintegrate into the community by many different ways such as improving academic and social knowledge, soft skills, cultural experiences, ... and help students understand the value of volunteerism.
